About Us

MarketForce is an operating system for retail distribution in Africa. Designed to capture and analyze customer data. It enables rapid and accurate customer service by processing;

  • Customer orders
  • Analyzing demographics
  • Helping merchants with operational efficiency

MarketForce offers unified payments and distribution POS solution, enabling the retailers to effectively handle complex digital processes while empowering them with full visibility and control over their businesses.
